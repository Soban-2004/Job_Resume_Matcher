Job Title: Data Analyst

Job Description:
We are looking for a detail-oriented Data Analyst to join our growing analytics team. The ideal candidate will have experience in analyzing complex datasets, generating insights, and supporting data-driven decision-making.

Responsibilities:

Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets from multiple sources.

Build dashboards and reports using tools like Tableau, Power BI, or Streamlit.

Collaborate with stakeholders to define KPIs and track business performance.

Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) and apply statistical methods.

Work with data engineering teams to optimize data pipelines.

Requirements:

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field.

Proficiency in Python (pandas, NumPy, matplotlib, scikit-learn).

Strong knowledge of SQL for querying relational databases.

Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, Streamlit).

Familiarity with machine learning concepts is a plus.

Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.

Experience in Cloud service Platforms.
